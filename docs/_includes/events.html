Hacktoberfest & GrimoireLab

Help driving growth of open source and make positive contributions to the community! This is how participants from all skill levels can help:

  1. Visit hacktoberfest website and sign in with your github account: Once linked, hacktoberfest will send you an email confirmation that you have officially been registered.
  2. Choose your favourite Grimoirelab issue and submitt your Pull Request: issues will be tagged using Hacktoberfest label.
  3. Wait for your PR to be merged: You will need a minimum of 4 Pull Requests merged to fully complete the hacktoberfest challenge.
