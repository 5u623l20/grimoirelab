Hacktoberfest & GrimoireLab

Help driving growth of open source and make positive contributions to the community! This is how participants from all skill levels can help:

  1. Visit hacktoberfest website and sign in with your github account.
  2. Choose your favourite GrimoireLab repository which is participating (labelled with the topic hacktoberfest).
  3. The beginner-friendly issues will be labelled using hacktoberfest, find a suitable issue and submit your pull request.
  4. Participate in the review process and wait for your PR to be merged. Ask the maintainers and get your PRs labelled accordingly.
